About the Project

Designing a new service to deploy landing pages across Tempur-Pedic’s retail network

This project demonstrates how I evolved a landing page service that my employer Icon Marketing provided to mattress retailers on behalf of Tempur-Pedic. For brand overview landing pages, Icon would design and deliver PDF and Photoshop artwork of customized templates for retailers to implement. While this worked for larger mattress retailers, small and mid-sized retailers that did not have a relationship with a website developer had difficulties deploying artwork into a functioning web page. My solution was to develop these landing pages in HTML & CSS in-house and create a scalable plan for deployment across Tempur-Pedic’s retail network.
