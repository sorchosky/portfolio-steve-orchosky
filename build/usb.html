Challenge

Connect the top mortgage lender with home buyers in the midwest

Union Savings Bank serves Ohio, Indiana, Kentucky, and Pennsylvania as having some of the most competitive closing costs in the region. The team at USB came to me at Icon Marketing to design and develop a new Wordpress website to showcase their dedication to the communities they serve.

Solution

A bank website with an emphasis on community

When determining the visual style, I placed emphasis on community through featuring faces in photography and emphasizing diversity with stock photo actors. One of the most important components of the website is the “We’re known for our low closing costs” section on the homepage. The previous iteration of the website did not feature closing costs because of potential legal concerns. Once I validated what numbers we could show, and included a user interaction to change the costs by region, USB gave the go-ahead to emphasize this key feature of the design.

Additionally, I added a new graphic element to the asides with a diagonal that matched the skew of the type in the USB logo.
