Raynor Gaming

Ecommerce website for professional gaming chairs

About the Project

Next-level innovation with the most advanced gaming chairs around

With a rich background in office chair manufacturing, Raynor came to Icon looking to take their knowledge of chair making into the competitive gaming industry. At Icon, I worked very closely with the team to help develop the brand into the digital space. With brand new products, there was no existing media or imagery. This gave us the exciting challenge to develop a visual and textual tone of voice. We interpreted that style with a photoshoot, with the final goal being an e-commerce website to market and sell the chairs.

My Role

Art direction, UI design, front-end development (Wordpress & WooCommerce)

Context

Website design & development for Raynor Gaming, a client of Icon Marketing
