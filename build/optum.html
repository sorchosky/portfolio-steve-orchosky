Brand Design

Well21 Brand Identity & Style Guide

Our team views MHCI’s capstone as a project that will have meaning to us long after we graduate. Recognizing this early in our project, our team wanted to create a professional brand that could elevate us from a student group to upcoming industry leaders.

Our goal for the team brand was to create an identity that could fit within the Optum service ecosystem, but not limit us too early into the project. Additionally, we wanted the brand to quickly convey our partnership with ProtectWell when talking with other experts in Optum, so our brand needed to complement their visual style.

We liked Well21 as our own take on ProtectWell because dropping “Protect” allowed us to place a new emphasis on general wellness, not just illness protection. The 21 has a surface meaning of our team in graduating class of 2021, but also hints at the behavioral science theory of 21 days to develop a new habit.
