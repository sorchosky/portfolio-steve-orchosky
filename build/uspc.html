U.S. Playing Card

Website design for the leading playing card company in the U.S.

About the Project

Stacking the deck for the leaders of the game

US Playing Card is the parent company of many different playing card brands, most notably Bicycle cards. For this project, I was responsible for a brand exploration, as well as designing and developing a Wordpress website for them during my employment at Icon Marketing. Their goal was twofold: to tell the story and history of the USPC and to connect with casinos via articles and trade shows. Shortly after this website went live, the U.S. Playing Card Company was acquired by Cartamundi, an international board and card game manufacturer based out of Belgium.
